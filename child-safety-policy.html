Child Safety Policy – Metenos

At Metenos, we are committed to protecting children and preventing exploitation, abuse, or any form of harm.

Our application:

  • Does not allow user-generated content involving minors.
  • Does not support user profiles with photos of children.
  • Has no features for uploading or sharing images, videos, or messages involving children.
  • Is regularly reviewed for compliance with safety standards.

If we become aware of any content or behavior violating these principles, we will take immediate action, including removing the content, banning the user, and reporting to relevant authorities.

To report abuse, please contact us at: support@metenos.pl.
